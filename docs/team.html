Meet the Project Team!

Dylan Leonard

is a senior history major with a love for railroads and industrial history. As it ties into the US's rapid urbanization at the end of the 19th century, How The Other Half Lives is a landmark work that speaks to society's shift away from an agrarian way of living. I can typically be found researching niche topics, fixing antique equipment, or enjoying the hobby of model railroading. While the scenes that Riis photographed are filled with people, much of my own photography reflects the beauty of the manmade world and the absence of life in decaying industrial zones.
