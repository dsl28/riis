Links of Interest: Browse a collection of related sources connected to Riis and life in the tenements.

Tenement Museum: 97 Orchard Street on the Lower East Side in New York is a historic tenement building used by the museum to preserve the stories of the ordinary people that once lived there from the 1860s to the 1930s.

New York Public Library: The NYPL provides an overview of the city's tenement problem and provides a collection of links with material from their collection and partner institutions to learn more.

Project Gutenberg: This site is home to a wealth of free book-length texts, 75,000 and counting. This includes the title covered here!

International Center of Photography: The ICP presents a collection of Riis's photography, which went largely unnoticed until a 1947 exhibition at the Museum of the City of New York. Today, these are acclaimed works of great signifigance.
